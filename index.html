Family Rules for Alphas

  1. You serve the family first. Personal desires come second.
  2. You mate who you are told to mate. Love is irrelevant.
  3. You maintain the image. A Hollis Alpha is strong, dominant, controlled, and above scandal.
  4. You do not show weakness. Submission, vulnerability, or emotional dependency are unacceptable.
  5. You do not expose family business. Loyalty is absolute.
  6. You do not leave. There is no walking away from the Hollis name.
